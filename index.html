Instructions:

  1. There are two pages, linked above. Open each one in a new window.
  2. The scores will be linked in all open windows. The Party Display page can be displayed on a TV, for example.

Notes:

  • The Party Display page currently only looks right when the browser window has a 16x9 aspect ratio.
  • Scores are saved, even if the computer restarts. But they're not online, so different devices and different browsers won't have linked scores.
  • To change a window from Party Display to Score Entry or vice versa, reload the page use the Back button.
  • You may see a surprise every few minutes.
